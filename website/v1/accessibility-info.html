Accessibility Support

Universal Eye-Tracking & Keyboard Control Hub

At 4weird Games, we are committed to making our gaming library playable for everyone. Through our partnerships and for our disabled gamer community, we have built built-in eye tracking, adaptive controls, and styling overrides that work directly in the browser with no special hardware required.

👁️ Eye Tracking Control (WebGazer.js)

Webcam Gaze Tracking

By enabling Eye Tracking in our settings menu (click the ♿ icon on the bottom right or navigation bar), we load WebGazer.js to map your eyes' position using a standard webcam.

Dwell-to-Click

You can interact with buttons, links, and game canvases simply by looking at them. The system monitors your gaze, and if you focus on an interactive element for the configured Dwell Time (adjustable between 400ms and 2000ms), a virtual mouse click is triggered automatically.

How to Calibrate

When you start eye tracking for the first time, a calibration overlay will guide you. Click each of the 9 red points on the screen 5 times while looking directly at them. When all dots turn green, calibration is stored and active.

⌨️ Full Keyboard Navigation

Interactive Focus Indicators

All clickable items, dropdowns, links, and game templates have high-contrast neon borders that appear when tabbed to, ensuring you always know where keyboard focus is located.

Game Key Mapping

For disabled gamers who prefer using the keyboard over a mouse, we support standard keys universally across all gameplay templates:

  • Use Tab and Shift + Tab to navigate site options.
  • Inside games, use Arrow Keys or W A S D (which are automatically mapped to arrow directions).
  • Press Spacebar or Enter to activate actions or triggers.
  • Press P or Escape to pause games.

⚙️ Styling Overrides & Settings

Click the floating ♿ button to adjust your options:

  • Reduced Motion: Disables background particle starfields, CSS transitions, and fast animations.
  • High Contrast Mode: Switches layouts to pure black and high-contrast yellow/white borders.
  • Text Size: Scales the text size up to 1.5x larger.
  • Dyslexia Friendly Font: Adjusts page typeface, kerning, and line heights for reading comfort.
  • Colorblind Filters: Simulated matrices for Protanopia, Deuteranopia, and Tritanopia.
  • Game Speed: Slow down games to 0.5x speed or increase to 1.5x speed to match your needs.
