About Sound Painter II

Welcome to a fully loaded music workstation. You have 4 synthesizers/tracks playing in perfect lockstep sync. Edit one track at a time by clicking channels on the left.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space - Play / Stop
  • C - Clear Active Grid
  • R - Reset Studio
  • Click - Toggle note step

Synthesis Guide

Lead Synth: High-pitched solo voice. Try changing its wave to square for a chiptune feel!

Sub Bass: Rich low-end. High filter cutoff gives crunchy bass; low cutoff gives clean sub.

Pad Synth: Rich chord accompaniment triggered automatically from chords root notes.

Drum Machine: Percussive synth voices. Kick, Snare, Hi-hat, and Clap elements.
