How to Play

Pick a route, choose a car, then race through pseudo-3D traffic. Finish well to earn coins, unlock faster cars, and open later routes.

Controls

  • Space / Shift / N - Nitro
  • P / Esc - Pause
  • R - Restart route

Progression

Complete routes to unlock Urban Sprint and Neon Night. Earn coins to buy faster cars in the garage.
