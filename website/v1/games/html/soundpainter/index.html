How to Play

Click any tile to paint it with sound and color. Each column is a note, each row is an instrument. Create patterns and hit Space to hear your creation.

Controls

  • Click - Paint tiles
  • R - Reset grid
  • Space - Play all
  • P / Esc - Pause music
