🕶️ The Silent Assassin Protocol

  • Target Assassinations: Each floor contains red-haloed **VIP Target Scientists**. Eliminate them to retrieve the exit keycard.
  • Disguise Systems: Press **E** near neutralized guards to steal their security uniforms. Guards won't detect you unless you run, attack, or meet **Enforcer Guards** (marked with a white dot over their heads).
  • Hiding Bodies: Neutralized guards leave bodies. If another guard spots a body, they sound a permanent alert. Stand near a body, press **G** to drag it, and walk to a metal **Dumpster Bin** to hide it forever.
  • Sound Distractions: Press **Right-Click** to toss a coin. Nearby guards hear it and leave their patrol routes to inspect the noise, setting up perfect sneak executions.
  • Silent Assassin Rating: Retain your rating by eliminating *only* the targets, hiding all bodies, and avoiding alarms. Non-target kills incur severe penalties!
