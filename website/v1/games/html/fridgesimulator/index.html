About the Creator

Founder of 4weird Games. Fridge Simulator emerged from my interest in systems thinking and resource management games. The darkly humorous premise - managing global food logistics with real consequences - exemplifies the 4weird approach to game design: take a serious concept, make it playable, and let players discover the depth for themselves.
