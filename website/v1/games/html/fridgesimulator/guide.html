🌍 MANAGED COUNTRIES

Each country has distinct population sizes, budget contributions, and food preferences. Unlock them as the days progress.

🇺🇸
USA
Unlocked: Day 1
Large family with a substantial budget but unhealthy preferences.

The USA has a family size of 4. They pay up to $300 in taxes each day when all members are alive. Their dietary preferences are fast food items: burgers, pizzas, hotdogs, fries, and donuts. Fulfilling their requests yields high financial returns.

🛒 FOOD SUPPLIERS

Procure ingredients from various suppliers. Balancing price, stock types, and quality is key to efficiency.

🏪
Grocery Store
Reliable Standard
Always fully stocked at standard prices. Perfect reliability.

The standard Grocery Store has a 1.0x price multiplier and holds all catalog items. Freshness is always 100%. Use it when you need specific items to fulfill family requests or plug severe nutritional gaps.

🗑️
Dumpster
Risky Freebie
Free food! However, stock is random and freshness starts at 50%.

Dumpster diving costs $0. However, the items are completely random, and they spawn partially spoiled (50% freshness). Food will rot quickly, and rotten food (0%) increases sickness. Use only in financial emergencies.

🍏 FOOD & NUTRITION

Each day, families consume food from their local fridge. Food belongs to one of five nutritional groups, which prevents malnutrition.

🍗
Proteins
Group 1
Includes meats, fish, eggs, and cheese. Critical for baseline stamina.

Proteins prevent rapid health degradation. Keep the Protein bar filled to avoid severe starvation penalties. USA and Japan families consume high amounts of proteins.

❄️ FRIDGE UPGRADES

Upgrade your storage system to optimize logistics and prevent food waste.

❄️
Deep Freezer
Anti-Spoilage
Slows food spoilage rate in all fridges by 50%.

Purchase the Deep Freezer upgrade to halve the speed at which food items decay. Highly recommended when stocking up via wholesale buying or when holding surplus dumpster food.

📰 DAILY EVENTS & STRATEGY TIPS

💡 Quick Tip: Always pay attention to the daily news events. A supply chain event can make certain food groups extremely expensive. Plan ahead by stockpiling food items during discount events!
