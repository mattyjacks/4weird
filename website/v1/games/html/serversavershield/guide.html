🧑‍💼 BUSINESS MANAGEMENT ZONE

Fly down to the bottom of the screen (75-82% of canvas height) where the 3 business people emojis are to enter the Management Zone. The game pauses, allowing you to review analytics, hire/fire staff, and check customer reviews.

How to Enter

Move your shield down to the Management Zone (below the "MANAGE BUSINESS" text). The game will automatically pause and open the Business Management overlay. To exit, click the "RESUME GAME" button or press ESC.

Management Tabs

⚔️ Combat Analytics
View enemies defeated by type, damage dealt/taken, accuracy percentage, peak combo, and waves survived.
💼 Business Analytics
Track revenue per second, total staff costs, net profit/loss, lifetime earnings, and severance tracking.
⭐ Reviews
See your average star rating (1-5), recent customer reviews, and rating breakdown by category.
👥 Staff Management
Hire new staff members or fire existing ones. When firing, choose to pay severance (ethical) or skip it (reputation penalty).

Severance System

When firing staff, you have two options:

Option A: Pay Severance (Ethical)
Cost: salary × 60 seconds
Effect: No reputation penalty

Option B: Skip Severance (Unethical)
Cost: FREE
Effect: -5 reputation per staff fired, 5% chance of lawsuit (-$1000, -10 rep)

Your ethics are tracked in the Business Analytics tab showing total severance paid vs skipped, and number of unethical firings.

🧑‍💼 Management Tip: Use the Management Zone between waves to assess your situation! Check if you can afford new staff, review which enemies are causing problems, and read customer feedback. The game pauses completely, so take your time making strategic decisions. Remember: firing staff without severance damages your reputation, which directly reduces your income!

⚙️ GAME MECHANICS

💰 Economic System

Starting Balance: $1000
Income Generation: Each ONLINE server generates money/sec based on customer count
Customer Trust: 0-100% multiplier on all income (at 50% trust, you earn half revenue)
Staff Salaries: Deducted every second from your balance
Bankruptcy: Game Over if balance stays negative for 10 seconds

🖥️ Server Management

3-Server Defense Line: Servers positioned at bottom of screen (20%, 50%, 80% width)
Server States: ONLINE (100% contribution), DEGRADED (under 30 HP, still online), OFFLINE (0 HP, no contribution)
Income Impact: 3 servers = 100% income, 2 servers = 70% income, 1 server = 40% income, 0 servers = 10% income
Server Health: Damaged by enemies reaching bottom of screen, heals via Emergency Repair ($300)
HP Bars: Visual indicators above each server showing health (green → yellow → red)

💻 Shop Zone

Location: Bottom of screen (85-95% of canvas height)
Items: Weapon Upgrade ($500), Emergency Repair ($300), Damage Boost ($200)
Interaction: Fly down to 3 laptop emojis and click to purchase
Instant: Purchases apply immediately, deduct from balance

🧑‍💼 Management Zone

Location: Bottom of screen (75-82% of canvas height)
Entry: Fly down to business people emojis (🧑‍💼👨‍💼👩‍💼)
Pause: Game pauses automatically when you enter
Tabs: Combat Analytics, Business Analytics, Reviews, Staff Management
Exit: Click "Resume Game" or press ESC

🎯 Combat System

Bullet Damage: Base 1 HP per bullet
Difficulty Multiplier: Easy 1.3x, Hard 0.8x
Companion Bonus: +1.5x damage when active
Pen Tester Bonus: +1.5x damage when hired
Combo System: Consecutive kills multiply score (max 5x multiplier)

🌊 Wave System

Wave Duration: 600 frames base, increases by 50 frames per wave
Wave Bonus: Wave number × 50 points when wave completes
Enemy Scaling: Speed increases by 10% per wave
Victory Condition: Survive 10 waves with at least 1 server ONLINE

📊 Difficulty Settings

Easy Mode: 0.7x enemy spawn rate, 1.3x player damage, lower ransom amounts
Hard Mode: 1.5x enemy spawn rate, 0.8x player damage, higher ransom amounts, more frequent ransomware

💯 REPUTATION SYSTEM

Your company's reputation affects customer acquisition, pricing power, and overall business success. Manage it carefully!

How Reputation Works

Starting Reputation: 50%
Range: 0-100%
Income Multiplier: Reputation directly affects your earning potential

  • 0% Reputation = 50% income multiplier (half earnings)
  • 50% Reputation = 75% income multiplier (75% earnings)
  • 100% Reputation = 100% income multiplier (full earnings)

Reputation Damage by Enemy

Different enemies damage your reputation when they hit your servers:

  • Social Engineer - 8 damage (most damaging - spreads rumors)
  • Fraudster - 7 damage (financial fraud damages trust)
  • Ransomware - 6 damage (data breaches hurt reputation)
  • DDOS Attack - 5 damage (service outages reduce trust)
  • Intelligence Agency - 4 damage (espionage concerns)
  • Cryptominer - 4 damage (resource theft damages brand)
  • Trojan Horse - 3 damage (malware concerns)
  • Black Hat - 2 damage (minor hacking attempts)
  • AI Pen Tester - 1 damage (legitimate security testing)

Reputation Recovery

PR Officer (📢) - Regenerates reputation +2% per second
Security Trainer (🎭) - Regenerates customer trust +1% per second
Article Writer (✍️) - Reduces Social Engineer reputation damage by 50% (counters rumors)
Compliance Officer (⚖️) - Reduces Fraudster reputation damage by 40% (prevents fraud)

💡 Reputation Tip: Hire a PR Officer early if you're taking heavy reputation damage. The +2/sec regeneration adds up quickly and directly increases your income. At 100% reputation, you earn 50% more than at 50% reputation!

🖥️ COMPUTE SYSTEM

Track your infrastructure utilization as enemies consume compute resources. This is a secondary scoring metric.

What is Compute?

Compute represents the computational resources your servers use to defend against and process threats. Each enemy type consumes different amounts of compute when defeated:

Compute Usage by Enemy

  • DDOS Attack - 200 compute (massive resource drain)
  • Cryptominer - 180 compute (steals processing power)
  • Ransomware - 150 compute (encryption overhead)
  • Intelligence Agency - 120 compute (complex threat)
  • Fraudster - 100 compute (transaction processing)
  • Trojan Horse - 80 compute (malware overhead)
  • Black Hat - 50 compute (simple attack)
  • Social Engineer - 60 compute (investigation overhead)
  • AI Pen Tester - 40 compute (lightweight testing)

Scoring

Your Max Compute Used is displayed on the game over and victory screens as a secondary score metric. Higher compute usage means you faced more/tougher threats and defended against them successfully.

💡 Compute Tip: Compute is just a metric - it doesn't affect gameplay directly. Focus on eliminating threats quickly to keep your servers running smoothly. The compute score rewards aggressive play and wave progression!

⭐ CUSTOMER REVIEW SYSTEM

Your business receives customer reviews based on your performance. Reviews affect your reputation, which directly impacts your income. Manage your reputation carefully!

How Reviews Work

150 Total Reviews: The system maintains up to 150 reviews in a circular buffer (oldest removed when full)
Review Generation: New reviews appear every 30 seconds of gameplay, plus immediately after server breaches
Star Rating: 1-5 stars based on your current reputation percentage
Customer Personas: 30 unique customer types (Enterprise, SMB, Startup, Individual)
Review Categories: Security (40%), Pricing (30%), Support (20%), Reliability (10%)

Star Rating Formula

⭐ (1 Star)
Reputation < 30%
⭐⭐ (2 Stars)
Reputation 30-50%
⭐⭐⭐ (3 Stars)
Reputation 50-70%
⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4 Stars)
Reputation 70-85%
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 Stars)
Reputation 85%+

Review Triggers

Regular Reviews: Generated every 30 seconds of active gameplay
Breach Reviews: Immediately after a server is hit by an enemy
Negative Reviews: Automatically 1-2 stars when server breaches occur
Good Reviews: 4-5 stars generated when reputation is high (85%+)
Reputation Impact: Each 5-star review gives +0.1 reputation

Viewing Reviews

Access your reviews through the Management Zone (fly down to the business people emojis). The Reviews tab shows:

• Average Rating: Your overall star rating (1.0 - 5.0)
• Recent Reviews: Last 3 reviews received
• Rating Breakdown: Count of 5★, 4★, 3★, 2★, and 1★ reviews
• Category Analysis: Which areas customers comment on most

⭐ Review Management Tip: Monitor your reviews closely! If you see many 1-2 star reviews, take action immediately: hire a PR Officer to regenerate reputation (+2% per second), or check if you need the Article Writer (counters Social Engineer reputation damage) or Compliance Officer (counters Fraudster damage). Remember: reputation directly affects income multiplier. At 100% reputation, you earn 50% more than at 50% reputation!

🧠 STRATEGY TIPS & TACTICS

Early Game (Waves 1-3)

• Focus on eliminating Black Hat Hackers and AI Pen Testers
• Accumulate balance before hiring staff
• Don't hire staff yet - your initial income is sufficient
• Collect Heal powerups to build up your balance
• Avoid getting hit - focus on dodging

Mid Game (Waves 4-6)

• Hire Firewall Tech ($200) when DDOS attacks appear (Wave 4)
• Consider hiring Incident Responder ($300) when ransomware appears (Wave 5)
• Fraudsters appear in Wave 6 - prioritize eliminating them
• Buy a second server if you have $1500+ balance
• Use Rapid Fire powerups to clear waves faster

Late Game (Waves 7-10)

• Hire CISO ($400) when Social Engineers appear (Wave 7)
• Hire Pen Tester ($280) for increased damage output
• Use backup servers strategically - link them to absorb ransomware
• Maintain customer trust above 70% - use PR Campaign upgrades
• Save Nuke powerups for waves with multiple high-value enemies
• Keep balance positive - bankruptcy is instant game over

Economic Strategies

Aggressive Economy: Buy servers early, hire multiple staff, maximize income generation
Defensive Economy: Hire staff to reduce threat damage, use backup servers, focus on survival
Balanced Approach: Buy 2-3 servers, hire 2-3 staff members, balance offense and defense
Bankruptcy Prevention: Always maintain positive balance - fire staff if necessary to avoid negative balance

Combat Tactics

• Prioritize high-threat enemies: DDOS, Ransomware, Fraudsters
• Use Rapid Fire powerups to burst down high-HP enemies
• Keep moving - don't stay in one spot
• Focus on eliminating threats before they reach servers
• Use Nuke powerups when overwhelmed by multiple threats
• Companion allies help clear waves - collect them when available

Staff Hiring Priority

1. Firewall Tech - Reduces DDOS damage (Wave 4+)
2. Incident Responder - Handles ransomware (Wave 5+)
3. CISO - Protects trust (Wave 7+)
4. Pen Tester - Increases damage (any time)
5. SOC Analyst - Detects stealth (optional, Wave 3+)
6. Product Team - Hire mid-game for revenue boost (Programmer, UX Designer, PM)
7. Tech Lead - Hire last for force multiplier effect

Shop & Management Zone Tips

• Emergency Repair ($300): Buy when any server drops below 30 HP (yellow)
• Weapon Upgrade ($500): Purchase early in Wave 1-2 for maximum value
• Damage Boost ($200): Use before boss waves (5, 10, 15) or when overwhelmed
• Management Zone: Enter between waves to assess staff needs and check reviews
• Severance Ethics: Pay severance when possible to protect reputation; only skip if desperate

Reputation Management Strategy

Reputation is Critical: It directly multiplies your income. At 50% reputation, you earn 75% of your potential. At 100% reputation, you earn 100%.
Protect Reputation: Hire Article Writer to counter Social Engineers (8 damage each). Hire Compliance Officer to counter Fraudsters (7 damage each).
Recover Reputation: Hire PR Officer ($350, $9/sec) to regenerate +2% reputation per second. This pays for itself quickly through increased income.
Priority: If reputation drops below 30%, prioritize hiring PR Officer. The income boost will quickly recover your balance.

Compute Score Optimization

What is Compute? A secondary score metric tracking infrastructure utilization. Higher compute = tougher threats defeated.
Maximize Compute: Focus on defeating high-compute enemies (DDOS = 200, Cryptominer = 180, Ransomware = 150).
Wave Progression: Reaching Wave 10 with high compute usage shows excellent threat management.
Scoring: Compute doesn't affect gameplay - it's just a bragging right metric for how much you've defended against!

Advanced Staff Synergies

Defensive Stack: Firewall Tech + Incident Responder + CISO = Handles all major threats
Economic Stack: Solutions Architect + PR Officer + Vendor Manager = Maximize income and reduce costs
Reputation Stack: PR Officer + Article Writer + Compliance Officer = Protect against reputation damage
Hybrid Stack: Pen Tester + Firewall Tech + PR Officer = Offense + Defense + Economy balance

🎯 Pro Tip: The key to winning is balancing offense and defense. You need enough damage output to clear waves quickly, but also enough economic protection to survive the financial drain of staff salaries. Don't hire staff you can't afford - bankruptcy is instant game over. Monitor your balance constantly and be ready to fire staff if you're going negative. Remember: reputation directly affects income, so protecting it is as important as protecting your servers!
