🏰 DEMOLITION TARGETS

As a modern demolition contractor, your goal is to destroy target properties. Direct your skeleton army to crush them while dodging defenses.

⛪
Old Church
Phase 1 Target
Standard brick structure. No defenses, easy to crush.

The Old Church has 300 HP and does not fight back. Great for leveling up early. Skeletons deal passive damage to it. Destroying it advances the game to Phase 2.

HP
300
🏭
Factory
Phase 2 Target
Reinforced factory. Drops falling bricks.

The Factory has 500 HP and active defenses. It periodically drops falling bricks (🧱) that damage the player on impact. Dodge them to stay alive.

HP
500
🏙️
Skyscraper
Phase 3 Boss
Giant skyscraper boss. Spawns guards and shoots missiles.

The Skyscraper is the final boss with 1000 HP. It spawns city guards to defend it and periodically launches rocket barrages. It displays a boss health bar at the top center of the screen.

HP
1000

⚡ NECROMANCY SPELLS

Use your spell power to summon minions and directly blast guards and buildings.

💀
Skull Spell
Mana Cost: 10
Fires a necro-bolt dealing base 15 damage.

Your primary ranged attack. Fires a flying skull in the direction of the cursor. Deals damage to guards and buildings. Spends 10 mana.

🦴
Summon Skeleton
Mana Cost: 25
Summons a skeleton minion that attacks buildings.

Summons a loyal skeleton that moves towards the active target building and attacks it repeatedly. Skeletons also distract and fight nearby city guards.

⭐ LEVEL UP UPGRADES

Gather XP from destroying buildings and defeating city guards to choose upgrades.

🧪
Acid Skull
Offensive Upgrade
Adds damage-over-time poison to your primary spells.

Infuses your spells with corrosive acid. Enemies hit take damage over time, melting guards' shields and building defenses continuously.

🧪 BATTLE POWERUPS

Powerups occasionally drop from defeated city defenders. Collect them to turn the tide.

⚡
Mana Overload
Temporary Buff
Gives infinite mana for 5 seconds.

Instantly maxes your mana and halts all mana consumption for 5 seconds. Spam skull spells and summon skeletons at will during this period!
